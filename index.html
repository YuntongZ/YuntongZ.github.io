Biography

Education:
Yuntong Zhang is currently studying at Department of Computer Science, The University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA), majoring in computer science.
She received her Master's Degree at College of Computer Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2019, majoring in computer science.
She received her Bachelor's Degree at School of Information and Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2016, majoring in software technology.

Professional Skill:
Except for mastering the knowledge of the courses, Yuntong Zhang Obtained CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) certificate in 2016.
She won the national second prize of “Cisco networking academy cup” in 2015.
She joined the NetUnion of UESTC (2013-2016) and was responsible for maintaining campus network.
